Original group project

This project was carried out by a group of friends. We brainstormed about the idea, wrote the script, acted in the film, and then edited it togeher.

Project Details

  • The script is loosely based on the popular tv series Stranger Things
  • The most complicated part of the film was morphing the faces into monsters
  • I created the opening and closing credits to look like the ones used in the original film.

Public service film

This project was a public service promotional film about an effort to reclaim some of Mission Bay, San Diego, as wetlands.

Project Details

  • I wrote the script and provided most of the images for the film
  • I was able to find maps of the project online and use screen capture to save them
  • I narrated the audio for the film but I also included rolling titles, for greater comprehension
