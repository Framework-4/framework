Buttons 4

When adding buttons you can choose from several styles and four sizes.
We offer the rectangle button, rounded corner and an oval button as well
as a Submit, Reset and Input button in small, medium, large and extra large.


The classes for these buttons are:


.btn-sm        - Small  button
.btn-md        - Medium button
.btn-lg        - Large button
.btn-exlg      - Exlarge button
.btn-oval      - Oval
.btn-rdcnr     - Rounded corner button
