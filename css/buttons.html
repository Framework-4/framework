Buttons

When adding buttons you can choose from two styles and four sizes.
We offer the rectangle button and an oval button in small, medium, large and extra large.


The classes for these buttons are:


.button-sm    or  .button-oval-sm     - Small  button
.button-md    or  .button-oval-md     - Medium button
.button-lg    or  .button-oval-lg     - Large button
.button-exlg  or  .button-oval-exlg   - Exlarge button

Rectangle Buttons


  <button type="submit" class="button-sm"> small</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-md"> medium</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-lg"> large</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-exlg"> exlarge</button>

Oval Buttons


  <button type="submit" class="button-oval-sm"> small-oval</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-oval-md"> medium-oval</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-oval-lg"> large-oval</button>
  <button type="submit" class="button-oval-exlg"> exlarge-oval</button>
